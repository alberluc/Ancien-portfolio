My life

I was born on June 16th 1997 in a little city of Savoie which is called Saint Jean de Maurienne.

I grew in Saint Michel de Maurienne until my 18 years.

I did a french baccalaureate STI2D (Sciences et technologies de l'industrie et du développement durable) option SIN (Système informatique et numérique) in Saint-Jean de Maurienne at lycée Paul Heroult. I obtained it with honors.

After that, I did a DUT Informatique (Diplome universitaire technologique) in the beautiful city of Annecy.Now, I'm in second year in this schooling.

I got my driving licence.
